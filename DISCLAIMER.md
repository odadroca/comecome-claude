## Not medical advice / not a medical device

ComeCome is a food, nutrition, and routine **tracking** tool for families and the professionals who
support them. It is **not** a medical device and does **not** provide medical, nutritional,
diagnostic, or treatment advice.

- The nutrition, growth, and medication-timing **insights** are produced by **fixed, rule-based**
  logic (not AI, not individualised clinical judgement). They are informational summaries of the
  data you entered — not recommendations.
- Growth **percentiles** use public WHO/CDC reference data for context and are **not** a clinical
  assessment.
- Nothing in ComeCome should be used to diagnose, treat, or make medication decisions.
- **Always consult a qualified healthcare professional** (paediatrician, dietitian, prescriber) for
  medical or nutritional decisions — especially regarding children and medication.
- ComeCome is **not** for emergencies. In an emergency, contact your local emergency services.

## Children

ComeCome stores data about children, entered and managed by a responsible adult guardian. It is
intended to be operated by adults on behalf of children.
